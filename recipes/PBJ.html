Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A simple, yet delicious meal that anyone can make and enjoy!

I N G R E D I E N T S

  • Bread
  • Jelly, jam, or preserves
  • Peanut Butter
  • Butter

S T E P S

  1. Slightly toast the bread
  2. Butter the bread
  3. Set the bread on a dish or a paper towel
  4. Spread Jam on one side of the bread
  5. On the other side of the brad, spread the Peanut Butter
  6. Put the two pieces together and E N J O Y !
